Cognitive Aspects in Design

Understanding how human cognition affects interaction design.

Experiential vs. Reflective Cognition
Experiential Cognition

Fast, automatic, intuitive responses

  • Driving a familiar route
  • Reading a novel for pleasure
  • Playing a mastered video game
Reflective Cognition

Slow, deliberate, analytical thinking

  • Writing an academic paper
  • Learning a new programming concept
  • Designing a website layout

Design implication: Support both types - provide intuitive paths for routine tasks (experiential) and clear guidance for complex tasks (reflective).
